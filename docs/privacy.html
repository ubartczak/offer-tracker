Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Offer Tracker ("the Extension") is a browser extension that helps users track job applications. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Email address — used to create and identify your account.
  • Authentication tokens — JWT tokens stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage.local) to keep you logged in.
  • Job application data — job title, company name, location, salary range, application status, and notes that you choose to save.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your browsing history.
  • We do not collect any data from pages you visit unless you actively save a job offer.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the Extension — saving and displaying your job applications. Data is stored on a secure server and is accessible only to you via your account.

Data Storage

Job application data is stored on a backend server hosted on Railway (railway.app). Authentication tokens are stored locally in your browser and are never sent to any third party.

Data Deletion
